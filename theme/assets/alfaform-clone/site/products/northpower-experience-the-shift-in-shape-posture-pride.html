    Who is AlfaForm designed for?

    AlfaForm is built for men looking to slim down their appearance, regain body confidence, straighten their posture, feel more energized throughout the day, and look leaner under any outfit.

    How does the AlfaForm tank actually work?

    AlfaForm uses advanced Ionic Compression fabric engineered to activate your body's natural response. Its micro-weave fibers promote healthy blood flow and help you maintain an upright, alert posture from morning to night. With consistent wear, it delivers a visibly streamlined torso, better spinal alignment, and an overall boost in how you look and feel.

    How quickly will I notice results?

    Every body is different, but the majority of customers report a visible transformation within the first 30 days. For maximum impact, wear your AlfaForm daily and complement it with balanced nutrition and regular movement.

    What happens if my size doesn't fit?

    Not a problem at all. Simply contact us at contact@alfaform.com and our team will arrange an exchange right away.

    How long does shipping take?

    All orders typically arrive within 5 to 8 business days anywhere in the world. The majority of packages land well before the 8-day window, but we quote the longer timeframe to stay on the safe side.

    Can I throw my AlfaForm in the washing machine?

    Absolutely — just use a cold gentle cycle to preserve the compression integrity and extend the lifespan of the fabric.

    What is the AlfaForm made of?

    80% nylon, 20% spandex.
